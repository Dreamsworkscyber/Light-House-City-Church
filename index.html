About Us

Lighthouse City Church is dedicated to compassionate service, biblical teaching, and equipping people to follow Jesus. We welcome families, students, and everyone seeking spiritual growth.

Our Mission

To be a beacon of hope—reaching, serving, and discipling our city through worship, outreach, and community transformation.

History

Founded to serve the local neighborhood, Lighthouse City Church has grown through community programs, consistent prayer, and a heart for people in need. We continue to build on that legacy.
